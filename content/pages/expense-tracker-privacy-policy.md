**INTRODUCTION**

Expense Tracker ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

**DATA COLLECTION**

The App does NOT collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All data remains on your device.

**SMS ACCESS**

The App requests permission to read SMS messages solely to parse bank and UPI transaction alerts. This data is:

* Processed entirely on your device
* Never transmitted to any server
* Never shared with third parties
* Stored only in local device storage

**DATA STORAGE**

All expense data, categories, and settings are stored locally on your device using secure local storage. We do not have access to this data.

**NO ANALYTICS OR TRACKING**

The App contains:

* No analytics tools
* No advertising SDKs
* No user tracking
* No third-party data collection

**DATA DELETION**

You can delete all your data at any time by clearing the app data or uninstalling the app.
